What determines the ammount of FWS award that a student recieves?

The federal government subsidizes a maximum of 75% of students' earnings; the employer provides the remainder. The federal and university shares are adjusted to maximize the total eligable dollars available to students. the amount of FWS also depends on the student's financial aid award.
